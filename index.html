Executive Market Summary

Industry reports indicate that the market for Skim Milk Powder (SMP) is seeing active demand. Manufacturers report a high volume of new contracts, with prices firmly established between 2.20 and 2.30 €/kg. Competitive pricing for EU products on the global market continues to drive significant international interest.

A similar strengthening trend is observed in Whole Milk Powder (WMP). Market activities have increased, and the lower-end price offers previously available have dissipated, stabilizing the market at an average of 3.03 €/kg.

The Whey Powder market remains consistently tight. Supply for food-grade quality is limited at lower price points, leading to a stable-to-firm price environment. Most liquid whey is currently being diverted into derivative production, further restricting powder availability.

In the Cheese sector, specifically Cheddar and Semi-hard varieties, demand remains exceptionally high. Production facilities are operating at full capacity, yet stocks in ripening warehouses remain low due to high clearance rates. Prices for Cheddar are averaging 3.78 €/kg.
